# CodePilot AI Startup Roadmap
# Stage 1 — Public Beta Readiness (v6.0)

Duration
========
4–8 Weeks

Goal
====
Prepare CodePilot AI for its first public beta.

The objective is NOT to add major new AI features.

The objective is:

• Fast
• Stable
• Beautiful
• Easy to use
• Reliable

At the end of Stage 1, strangers should be able to use CodePilot AI without your help.
